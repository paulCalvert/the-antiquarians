Image Generation Prompts

Active prompts for current session imagery, noble house crests, and compact seals. Older character and NPC prompts are in the archive.

Setup — ChatGPT Image Generation

How to Generate Each Image
  1. Open ChatGPT and start a new conversation
  2. Paste the full prompt below — description + the base style line for that image type appended at the end
  3. For portraits: add "Generate as a tall portrait image (2:3 format)."
  4. For scenes: add "Generate as a wide landscape image (16:9 format)."
  5. For crests and seals: add "Generate as a square image."
  6. If the result is too photorealistic, follow up: "Make it more painterly, less like a photograph."

Portrait base style (characters and NPCs):
Painterly oil portrait illustration, dark fantasy setting, dramatic low candlelight, muted desaturated color palette, dark vignette background, bust portrait framing, highly detailed face. Avoid modern clothing, avoid anime or cartoon style, avoid 3D render or photograph, avoid deformed hands.

Heraldic crest base style (noble house crests):
Flat heraldic badge illustration, gothic dark fantasy aesthetic, circular seal or shield format, bold simplified symbolic design in a woodcut or linocut style, limited two-tone or three-tone color palette, highly iconic and recognizable at small sizes, dark background. Avoid photorealism, avoid complex scenes, avoid characters or figures, avoid 3D rendering.

Ancient seal base style (harbor compact seals):
Ancient stone relief carving, pre-Cataclysm aesthetic, worn slightly at the edges with age, deep shadow filling the carved channels, circular seal or badge format, bold simplified line work suggesting organic or architectural detail, dark background. Avoid color fills — monochrome stone grey and shadow only. Avoid modern aesthetics, avoid photorealism, avoid 3D rendering.

Scene illustration base style (session set pieces):
Painterly dark fantasy scene illustration, wide landscape format (16:9), dramatic low dim lighting with a single-source cold or warm light, muted desaturated color palette, cinematic atmospheric composition with strong depth, highly detailed environment. Avoid characters in the foreground, avoid anime or cartoon style, avoid 3D render or photograph.

NPCs — The Ash-Hand Compact

Use the portrait base style above. Save to img/npcs/[name].png

Nell Vask — img/npcs/nell-vask.png

A woman in her late forties in a worn leather coat with deep inner pockets, standing slightly angled as if she has just turned to look at something she was already watching. Hard practical features, dark hair pulled back without ceremony, expression giving nothing away. The right hand is visible at her side, palm partially turned outward — the skin across the palm marked with a pattern of old thin scars, all cuts running the same direction, accumulated over years of contract signings. She looks like a clerk who has survived things clerks are not supposed to survive. Dim lantern light from one side, the background indistinct.

Pale Jeren — img/npcs/pale-jeren.png

A slight man in a shabby coat that was once better quality, face belonging in the background of a harbor scene — forgettable features, receding chin, nervous eyes that don't quite meet the viewer's. Dry pale skin despite a faint sheen that suggests perpetual low-grade anxiety. One hand half-raised, caught mid-gesture of rubbing grey dust from his fingertips, a motion he does not appear to notice himself performing. The harbor visible behind him, out of focus. He is the kind of person you have seen a dozen times and cannot remember afterward.

The Handless Clerk — img/npcs/the-handless-clerk.png

A figure in a long dark coat, perfectly still, facing slightly away as if already mid-departure. The sleeves hang past where hands would be and are pinned shut at the cuffs — no hands visible, no suggestion of hands. A sealed document held between forearm and chest, since there are no hands to hold it. No expression visible beneath the brim of a plain hat. The corridor or passage behind him is already swallowing his outline. The impression is of someone who was just here and is already gone.

Palm-Mother Edda — img/npcs/palm-mother-edda.png

A very old woman crouched close to a wall in a dim passage, pressing a chalk handprint onto the stone near the floor — the motion deliberate, unhurried, complete. Layers of worn and mismatched clothing, grey-white hair loose. At her side on the floor: a small arrangement of a child's glove stuffed with grey ash, bent coins placed in a careful pattern. She is not looking at the viewer. Whether she is unaware of being watched or entirely aware and indifferent is impossible to determine. The space around her feels like somewhere she has always been.
