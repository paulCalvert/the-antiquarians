Threat

Setarra

Demon of Dust, Hands, and Deals. Patient. Distributed. She has found the crew.

Domain & Nature

  • Domain: Dust, hands, secrecy, contracts, obligation, debt
  • Tone: uncanny and patient — not seductive, not dramatic. She is distributed.
  • She does not reveal her full plan to any follower. No agent knows what the others are doing.
  • Cult doctrine: "Let not one hand know what the other is doing."

Appearance

A robed humanoid form — but the robe moves wrong. Too many hands and arms emerge from it, of varying sizes and proportions: some small as a child's, some elongated and wrong. They reach, withdraw, and perform tasks you cannot see. She is never still. Her approach is foretold by dusty handprints appearing on mirrors, floors, and walls — shadowing movements no one made.

Her face, if you can look directly at it without going mad, is two hands framing a single eyeball. This is also her icon.

Aliases

  • Lady of Hands
  • The Handmaiden
  • Lady of Dust

Summoning

The Mirror Ritual

Walk your hands across a mirror, whispering: "Grasping, holding, reaching, I unbind thee. Secrets, taboo teaching come and find me." If performed correctly, dusty handprints will appear in the mirror — shadowing your own hands, but wrong. You will be visited by Setarra.

The Three Phrases

Setarra can also be invoked through three spoken phrases, each beginning with a syllable of her name:

  • SET your gaze on me
  • TAboo knowledge, teach me
  • RApture me unto yourself

The dusty handprints come first. Then she does.

The Ash Compact (Her Cult)

  • Operates in independent cells — members do not know each other
  • Activities: acquiring ritual components, Faustian bargains, sabotage, infiltration
  • One cell was already encountered — the cultist stowaway on the ghost train (now dead)
  • Other cells are active in Doskvol and the crew will encounter them again

Her Plan

The Long Game

Setarra wants to hijack the harbor compact — not destroy the city, but control it. She will let some horrors through, manipulate tides, create fear and unpredictability. She needs a mortal intermediary to sign as a new compact holder: likely Jared, or his bastard son as a fallback.

How She's Using the Crew

She doesn't need to touch the crew directly. Scurlock is already doing the work for her — directing them to collect the three ritual components she needs. They think they're working against her. They are not.

Weakness

Antithetical to Water

Setarra is dust. The harbor is water. She cannot fully dominate the harbor — this metaphysical opposition is the key to her eventual downfall. The ghost train actively resisted her influence from within. The Watcher is her structural opposite. Whatever weapon eventually stops her will involve this fact.

Current Status

  • Has made direct contact with the crew — appeared at the Sunken City after the ghost train score
  • No longer operating through subtle traces only — she is openly aware and actively watching
  • Specifically focused on Harrow — she wants to reclaim him
  • Has at least one agent actively observing the crew's next moves
