The Watcher

Ancient. Bound. Doing its job. The city doesn't know it exists — but it would die without it.

What It Is

  • An ancient bound entity in Doskvol's harbor
  • Its function: keep leviathans and deep horrors out of the bay
  • Tone: a drowned sentinel — an ancient laborer transformed into a custodian. Grim and necessary. Not benevolent.
  • It has been doing this job for longer than the current city has existed
  • It is bound by the harbor compact — the six noble seals are its tethers as much as its authority

The Tide Tithe

Public Superstition

Citizens of Doskvol — especially gondoliers, sailors, and dockworkers — throw coins into the harbor water. They call this the Tide Tithe. They don't know why they do it. It's just what you do. This is an unconscious echo of the original compact obligations, passed down through generations as superstition until the meaning was lost. The coins mean nothing to the Watcher. The habit means something.

How It Communicates

  • Not words — images and sensations
  • Marine imagery: the harbor from below, deep water pressure, salt, barnacles
  • Primarily speaks to Costya — she has the clearest connection through the token
  • Also reaches Brick through his delayed reflections — passive attention, not active communication yet
  • Its presence peaks near the Drowned Ledger and in the Sunken Temple beneath the docks

The Metaphysical Contrast

Water vs. Dust

Setarra is dust. The Watcher is water. They are metaphysically opposed. The ghost train's resistance to Setarra's influence came partly from the Watcher's presence running through the route. In the Sunken Temple, the Watcher's influence is strongest — Setarra cannot operate there effectively. This opposition is the key to her eventual defeat.

Current Status

  • Still doing its job — the harbor is currently secure
  • Aware of the crew — noting them, recording them in its own way
  • Showed Costya the stakes during the Sunken Temple score: what the compact working looks like, and what failure looks like
  • If the compact is transferred to Setarra, the Watcher will be compromised — not destroyed, but wrong-commanded
