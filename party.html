Crew Advancement

How It Works — Plain Version

The crew earns XP by doing things that fit who they are: pulling off scores that match their type, trying risky things, expressing their rep, etc. When the XP track hits 6, the crew picks one advancement from the options below. Think of it as: the crew gets collectively better, adds resources, or expands its reach.

Currently at 4/6 XP. Two more good sessions and they can pick something.

Note on Hawking

The crew's original goal was running the Sunken City as an underground auction house for illegal artifacts — that's the Hawkers model. They haven't leaned into this much yet. Downtime is the natural time to push on it: running a sale, finding buyers, moving inventory. It's also their main legitimate income source.

Crew Assets — Uncle Jared's Contributions

Uncle Jared's Warehouse — Train Prowess

Access to Jared's warehouse provides a legitimate storage and staging location. Jared offered the arrangement himself — come work in his warehouse, bulk up some. He's glad to have the crew there. Mechanically grants access to Train Prowess — crew members can train Prowess actions during downtime here.

Uncle Jared's Goat Carriage Delivery — Upgrade Opportunity

Access to Jared's delivery carriage operation. Opportunity to upgrade the current cart into something more capable. However: the Cabbies (a Wraiths faction ally) may view a crew-operated delivery service as competition. Upgrading this asset could create friction with the Wraiths indirectly.

Bonnie & Clyde

The two goats who pull the crew's carriage. They are functional and, by all accounts, professional.

Crew Contact

Anya — Dilettante / Influencer

The crew's primary Hive-side contact. Social reach, upper-circle access, family ties to Alaric. The day-to-day relationship between the Antiquarians and The Hive runs through her. There is also an unspoken arrangement developing on a more personal level between her and Alaric that neither has quite named aloud.

Headquarters — The Sunken City

A hidden underground auction house beneath Nightmarket. Canal-accessible, built into buried ruins. Front business: Marge's Antique Shop — Marge is deadpan, chronically underpaid, and universally beloved.

The name is more literal than most clients realize. The HQ sits atop genuine pre-Doskvol ruins — the kind that predate the current city's construction. Some tunnels go deeper than they should.

Location: Nightmarket  |  Access: Hidden entrance via shop front  |  Security: Secure Level 1
