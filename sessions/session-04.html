Session 04

The Warehouse Heist

Score against House Valchek. The token. First contact with the conspiracy.

What Happened

  • Crew infiltrated a House Valchek warehouse, directed there by Scurlock via Jeeves
  • Stole a mysterious metal coin — 5 etched lines on its face (the train token)
  • Caused significant chaos: fire, a ghost incident
  • Escaped with the token

Key Outcomes

The Token

A worn metal transit token with 5 etched lines. Not obviously magical. Would prove to be the key to the ghost train — and when Costya touched it, the beginning of something she hasn't fully understood yet.

Consequences

  • Marla Vale (House Valchek) survived the score and began investigating the crew
  • Heat increased — the warehouse chaos drew attention
  • Alaric came under Valchek suspicion

Threads Advanced

  • Train token acquired — the key to the 5th Line
  • Made an enemy of House Valchek (Marla Vale investigation clock started)
  • Scurlock's first request fulfilled
  • The crew is now his instrument, whether they know it or not
