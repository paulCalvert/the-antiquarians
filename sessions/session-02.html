Session 02

The Crows' Happy Hour

Dusty handprints in a mirror. A note signed only "R." Setarra obliterated the bar.

Setup

  • The Crows run occasional "happy hours" — their smaller offices open pubs to the community to build relations and take on work
  • The crew went to gather intelligence on who ordered the rival gang to steal the Spirit Wardens outfit

What Happened

  • Light scuffle with the Crows inside — the people there did not recognize the Antiquarians
  • Flint (Lurk) located the original orders to the Crows to retrieve the Spirit Wardens outfit
  • The orders did not show the sender's name — only the logo of an unknown high-tier faction
  • The note was signed simply "R" — their contact signs only as "R"
  • Their contact is Rook — identity not yet established in-fiction
  • The crew did not recognize the faction's symbol

Harrow's Devil's Bargain

The Bargain & Its Cost

Harrow took a Devil's Bargain and failed on this score. The cost: he attracted Setarra's attention directly. First sign — dusty handprints appeared in a mirror. A progress clock began: Harrow escapes before Setarra arrives.

Setarra Arrives

The crew got out with their information before she got there. When Setarra arrived at the Crows' bar, she obliterated it. The building. The people inside. All of it. This was not subtle. This was a message — and it was very, very public.

Key Outcomes

Intelligence Gained

Someone above the Crows — a high-tier faction the crew doesn't recognize yet, operating through a contact signing only as "R" — wanted the Spirit Wardens outfit badly enough to contract the job. The faction logo is an open mystery.

Consequences

  • Score became very high-profile — Setarra destroying a building full of people is not quiet
  • Clock started: Setarra Finding Harrow — she's locked onto him now
  • Heat increase from the bar incident
  • The Crows lost people; they don't know why yet

Open Threads

  • The unknown faction and their contact "R" — who are they, and why did they want a Spirit Wardens outfit?
  • Setarra's clock on Harrow is now running
  • The Crows will eventually investigate what happened to their bar
