Session 01

The Silkshore Job

A tip from the Wraiths. An opium den in Silkshore. And a rival crew who wanted the same thing.

Setup

  • The crew heard from the Wraiths about an opportunity in Silkshore
  • Target: a Spirit Wardens outfit, still on a man who had overdosed in an opium den
  • A Spirit Wardens uniform is valuable contraband — ideal for auction

What Happened

  • Crew moved into the opium den in Silkshore to retrieve the outfit
  • Interrupted mid-score by a rival gang attempting to steal the same item
  • A light confrontation — no one died
  • The Antiquarians secured the Spirit Wardens outfit and got out
  • Determined afterward that the rival gang was associated with The Crows

Key Outcomes

The Spirit Wardens Outfit

A full Spirit Wardens uniform — rare, illegal to possess without authorization, and worth serious coin at auction. The crew brought it back to the Sunken City but have not yet put it up for sale.

Open Thread — Unsold Outfit

The Spirit Wardens outfit is still sitting in the Sunken City, not yet auctioned. Someone clearly wanted it badly enough to send a Crows-affiliated crew. What was it actually for? And who ordered the Crows to get it?

Threads Advanced

  • First contact with The Crows as an opposing faction
  • Spirit Wardens outfit acquired — valuable loose thread
  • The Wraiths established as a source of score opportunities
