Session 05

Train Access

Into the underground ruins. Summoning the 5th Line.

What Happened

  • Crew descended into underground ruins beneath the city
  • Navigated submerged tunnels and environmental hazards
  • Crossed a chasm
  • Defeated an altered Hull construct — repurposed for something it wasn't built for
  • Passed a ghost ticket-keeper
  • Found the terminal and summoned the ghost train

Key Outcomes

The Terminal

The mechanism for calling the 5th Line. The train runs on a schedule that no longer corresponds to any living timetable. Using the terminal pulled it back to something like a station.

Clock Results

Call of the Train: Filled → Level 1 Harm (Troubled) on the crew. Reach the Platform: Completed.

Consequences

  • Crew entered the next score with stress and Level 1 Harm — no downtime between sessions 5 and 6
  • The altered Hull construct suggested someone else had been down here before them

Threads Advanced

  • Accessed the ghost train infrastructure
  • Confirmed the 5th Line exists and can be summoned
  • Evidence that others have sought the train — and been stopped
