Session 03

Connections & Consequences

No score. The crew takes stock, makes allies, and receives a letter from someone they'll wish they'd never met.

Focus

A roleplay-heavy session without a formal score. The crew's priority after the bar incident: shake Setarra off Harrow's trail before her clock fills.

What Happened

  • Crew decided their immediate priority was getting Setarra off Harrow — the obliterated bar made clear she wasn't being subtle anymore
  • Alaric became increasingly obsessed with understanding the occult. He fears it, and he's decided ignorance is more dangerous than knowledge.
  • First meeting with Lady Crane — a solid and well-connected contact among the Gondoliers
  • The crew received their first letter from Lord Scurlock
  • Met Scurlock's major domo: Jeeves
  • Jeeves directed them to find the token being held in the Valchek warehouse — setting up the next score

New Contacts

Lady Crane — Gondoliers

A well-connected figure among the Gondoliers. A solid contact — reliable and knowledgeable about the waterways, the docks, and the people who move through both. The Gondoliers know things about the harbor that most factions don't.

Jeeves — Scurlock's Major Domo

Scurlock's man. Precise, formal, and entirely unreadable. He delivered Scurlock's instructions and directed the crew toward the Valchek warehouse and the token hidden there.

The Letter from Scurlock

What the Crew Doesn't Know

This letter was the beginning of Scurlock's use of the Antiquarians as his collection instrument. He presented himself as an interested patron. The crew had no reason to distrust him. The token he directed them to was the first piece of a very long play.

Threads Advanced

  • Lady Crane established as a Gondoliers contact
  • Scurlock and Jeeves introduced — crew is now on his radar and vice versa
  • Alaric's occult obsession begins — he will pursue this knowledge actively going forward
  • Next target established: the token in the Valchek warehouse (Session 04)
