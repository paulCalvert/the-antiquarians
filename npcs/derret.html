Grinders — Enforcer

Derret

Broad. Scarred. One arm ends in an axe. Hutton's enforcer and a very direct problem-solver.

Appearance

  • Squat, broad build — low center of gravity and an enormous amount of mass
  • Bald head with a visible dent — the skull was damaged at some point and it shows
  • Covered in scars — none of them slow him down
  • One arm has been replaced with a large axe extension — functional, brutal, unmistakable
  • Does not try to look less threatening than he is

Personality

  • Dull-witted — not the strategist; follows orders well and executes them completely
  • His value to the Grinders is physical, not tactical — and he knows it, and it's fine
  • Genuinely believes in Hutton's goal; the factory took something from him too

Role

  • Playbook: Cutter
  • Hutton's enforcer — the Grinders' physical presence and final word in disputes
  • Not mindless — he follows Hutton because he believes in the goal

Connections

  • Hutton — his leader
  • Sercy — the Grinders' lieutenant; they work in tandem
