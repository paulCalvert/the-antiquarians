Veleris Quill

Legal occultist. Bloodline hunter. She speaks to Jared as though his participation is merely a formality — because to her, it is.

Who She Is

The Archivist-Spy

A severe, meticulous operative who specializes in bloodlines, inheritance claims, hidden family branches, and legal blackmail. She always wears gloves. She speaks as though everything is already recorded — every name, every lineage, every obligation. Her presentation is that of a very precise, very patient solicitor. The cult activity is entirely invisible from outside.

Her fingertips are permanently greyed when she removes her gloves — ash-stained vellum, as though her skin has absorbed the records she works with. She does not explain this. She does not seem to notice.

Her Current Operation — Jared

She runs the Meridian Circle, the Ash Compact's active front in Brightstone. She has identified Jared Dalmorrian as the most accessible living compact signatory: well-meaning, susceptible to prestige, and hungry to feel historically significant. She approaches him not as a cult leader but as a scholar of noble genealogy — enthusiastic, knowledgeable, warm. She does not ask him to believe anything strange. She shows him documents. She treats his bloodline as though it carries legal weight, because it does.

She would never say "sign this for Setarra." She would say: "The records already confirm your family's connection to the original compact. This is simply a matter of formalizing what is rightfully yours."

How She Operates

Vibe at the Table

Cold. Patient. Surgically polite. She never raises her voice because she never needs to — the records speak for themselves, and she is simply the person who knows where they are. She makes Jared feel like he is being recognized for something real. She makes everyone else feel like they are already behind.

She treats every interaction as a negotiation whose outcome has already been decided. She is never hostile. She is never warm. She is simply proceeding.

If the Crew Encounters Her

She will not panic. She will not fight. She will assess. If confronted directly about the Meridian Circle, she produces documentation suggesting it is exactly what it claims to be — a scholarly society. The forgeries are excellent. The real records are elsewhere. She leaves a conversation having learned more than she gave away.

She is most dangerous when the crew thinks they've gotten ahead of her. She has three fallback plans. She always has three fallback plans.

Connections

  • Jared — primary current target; she knows exactly which buttons to press
  • The Ash-Hand Compact — Inner Ring; reports to the compact's senior structure, not to Setarra directly
  • Alaric — she knows the Dalmorrian seal covers both of them; she considers Alaric the complication, not the target
  • The Meridian Circle — her front operation; several members are her assets; one or two are genuine scholars who don't know what they've joined
