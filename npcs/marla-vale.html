Marla Vale

She survived the warehouse. She hasn't forgotten. She's been building her case quietly ever since.

Background

  • Investigator for House Valchek — a minor noble house that holds power through centuries of accumulated deeds, water rights, and legal instruments
  • Was present during the warehouse heist (Session 04) — survived the chaos
  • Has been running a slow-burn investigation into the Antiquarians since

The Threat

Active Investigation Clock

Marla is patient and methodical. She doesn't need to move fast — she needs to be right. The crew should feel this pressure intermittently: a contact who was questioned, a report that seems to have their fingerprints on it, someone asking about an antique shop in Nightmarket.

Pressure on the Crew

  • Alaric is specifically under Valchek suspicion — his noble background makes him a natural focal point
  • The longer the crew stays at Heat 7+, the faster her clock ticks
  • She is not a blunt instrument — she will try to leverage before she moves openly
