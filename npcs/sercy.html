Grinders — Lieutenant

Sercy

Hutton's lieutenant. One hand is calipers. The other is a hook. She doesn't need hands to be the most dangerous person in the room.

Appearance

  • Beautiful — in a way that tends to be the last thing people notice about her
  • No hands. One wrist ends in a set of calipers; the other ends in a hook.
  • Carries herself with complete comfort in her body — the augmentations are not a vulnerability

Personality

  • Defiant — she does not accept limitations, from herself or anyone else
  • Outspoken — says what she thinks; doesn't moderate for comfort
  • Savage — when it comes to a fight, there is no restraint

Role

  • Playbook: Prowler
  • Hutton's lieutenant — she executes his orders and runs Grinder operations day-to-day
  • Where Hutton plans, Sercy deploys

Connections

  • Hutton — her leader; deeply loyal
  • Derret — the Grinders' enforcer; works alongside her
