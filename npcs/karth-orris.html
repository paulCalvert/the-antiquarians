Karth Orris

Mercenary commander. Ruthless, insightful, and in love with a woman who was in love with someone else.

Appearance

  • Shaved completely bald Ś clean, deliberate
  • Chin beard Ś the only hair he keeps
  • Carries himself like a soldier, which he more or less is

Personality

  • Ruthless Ś his professional reputation; he gets results and doesn't flinch
  • Insightful Ś reads people and situations well; not just muscle
  • Jealous Ś specifically of Roric, Djera's former lover. Roric is dead now, which doesn't fully resolve the jealousy.

His Relationship with Djera

The Complication

Karth loves Djera Dalmer. He would do anything for her Ś and he has, repeatedly. But Djera's great love was Roric, the old Crows leader who was killed by Lyssa. Karth has always been second to a ghost, which makes him dangerous in specific ways. He is fiercely motivated to help Djera avenge Roric Ś partly for her, partly because killing the thing she loved clears the field.

Role in the Hive

  • Mercenary commander Ś he runs the Hive's muscle and enforcement operations
  • Djera's most loyal operative, though not through the normal channels of faction politics
  • The Hive's goal to avenge Roric runs through him Ś he is likely point on whatever that plan looks like
