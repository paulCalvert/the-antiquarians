The Crows

Mad Eye Jake

Massive, short-fused, two lazy eyes. He is sensitive about the eyes. Do not bring them up.

Appearance

  • Massive frame — physically imposing in a way that makes rooms feel smaller
  • Two lazy eyes, both drifting — the source of his name and a sore subject
  • Sensitive about the eyes. People who mention them regret it.

Personality

  • Short fuse — doesn't take much to set him off
  • Short attention span — gets bored, gets distracted, gets dangerous when he's either
  • Lower lieutenant — has authority but not patience or subtlety
  • Led the crew that tried to steal the Spirit Wardens outfit in Session 01; the Antiquarians beat him to it

His Grievances

Jake lost the Spirit Wardens outfit job to an unknown crew. Then Setarra destroyed his bar — people he knew, likely people he was responsible for. He doesn't know the Antiquarians were involved in either event. If that ever connects, a massive man with a short fuse and a grudge becomes a specific kind of problem.

Threat Level

Distant Thread

Jake is a loose thread, not an active threat. He's not hunting the crew. But he has reasons to be angry and the impulsiveness to act on them without thinking it through. In the current Crows turmoil after Lyssa's takeover, he's an unstable element in a faction that already has instability problems.
