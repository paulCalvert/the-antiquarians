Hutton

Skovlan tribal leader. Half machine. He kept himself alive long enough to come back for the people who did this to him.

Background

  • Skovlan — tribal leader from Skovland, not Doskvol-born
  • Playbook: Leech — alchemist and tinkerer
  • Worked (or was forced to work) in a leviathan blood processing factory in Skovland
  • The poisons and injuries from the factory have been slowly killing him for years
  • He has kept himself alive through mechanical and alchemical augmentation — his body is a ongoing project

Appearance

  • Eye patch over one eye
  • Shattered chest — his torso is a working machine: a vest studded with tubes, gauges, chemical reservoirs, and mechanical components that keep his damaged organs functioning
  • The augmentation is not clean or polished — it's survival engineering, done over years with what was available
  • Looks like a man who has been repaired rather than enhanced

Personality

  • Confident — he leads a faction of people who survived things that should have killed them; he carries that authority
  • Volatile — his calm can break suddenly; the factory damage runs deep in ways that aren't just physical

The Grinders

Who They Are

The Grinders are an amalgamation of disfigured and misshapen factory workers, augmented and held together by Hutton. People who survived what should have killed them — or didn't fully survive it — who have found each other under his leadership. They are not a conventional gang. They are a wounded community with a leader who has a plan.

The Plan

What Hutton Wants

A warship. Specifically, one capable of sailing back to Skovland and razing the leviathan blood factory that did this to him and his people. He is not hiding this goal. He has told the crew. He is working toward it methodically.

Relationship to the Crew

Current Standing — Square

The crew originally started at -1 with the Grinders because they acquired their hideout from them. Victor and Hutton met and connected over their shared background as alchemists/engineers. Hutton called them square. The crew has not yet done anything to help with the warship.

Open thread: Hutton is looking for a warship. The crew knows this. They have not acted on it. Whether they help, hinder, or stay out of it is unresolved.

Connections

  • Victor / Eyes — bonded over alchemy; the reason the crew is now square with the Grinders
  • Sercy — his lieutenant
  • Derret — his enforcer
