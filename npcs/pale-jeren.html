Pale Jeren

He arranges small bargains for desperate people. He does not ask what they’re for.

Who He Is

The Broker
  • Dockside broker who connects desperate people with what they need — for a price they don’t always understand until later
  • Shabby coat, nervous manner, and the kind of face that belongs in the background of a harbor scene
  • Always sweating somehow, despite dry skin. The body doesn’t quite add up.
  • Compulsively rubs grey dust from his fingertips. Doesn’t notice himself doing it.
  • Not a true believer — a professional facilitator who found a reliable income stream and has been in it long enough that getting out looks harder than staying
What He Knows

The Compact’s outer ring: who’s looking for what, which desperate people on the docks have been recently approached, what small bargains have been made in the last week. He’s a good source of low-level leads and “someone was asking about you” warnings.

He can be flipped. He has no real loyalty, just self-preservation. The right combination of pressure and alternative income would probably work. The problem is he would then tell the Compact the crew flipped him, because that also serves his self-preservation.

Connection to Harrow

He Would Know the Face

Jeren was a contact point for message handoffs and small errands during Harrow’s time in the Compact. Their paths crossed enough that Jeren would recognize Harrow on the docks. The reunion, if it happened, would be careful — two people who know something about each other and are assessing how much the other has changed.

GM Note

Use him as a canary and a complication. If the crew is being watched by the Compact, Jeren is how they find out — he might warn Harrow, for his own reasons, or he might report to Nell Vask, for the same ones. He’s not reliable in either direction. That’s his value as a recurring presence.

Connections

  • The Ash-Hand Compact — Petitioner-level broker; handles small bargains and low-level fieldwork on the docks
  • Harrow — former contact; knows his face; would recognize him at the docks
  • The Docks — his territory; he knows who moves through here and what they’re looking for
