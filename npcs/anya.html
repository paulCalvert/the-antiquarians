Anya Quellin

The Antiquarians' day-to-day Hive contact. An influencer with the right connections — and a complicated relationship with Alaric that nobody has fully defined yet.

Role

  • The actual working contact between the Antiquarians and The Hive
  • Influencer — someone who moves through Doskvol's social and commercial circles; knows people, makes introductions, shapes reputations
  • Probably the person who vouched for the crew when they aligned with the Hive

Connection to Alaric

House Quellin

Anya is the daughter of House Quellin's current head — new money, two-generation title, commercial wealth seeking the social weight that only an old name can provide. Her family wants the Dalmorrian connection; Alaric's family wants the Quellin fortune. The arrangement is understood on both sides. Whether Alaric and Anya share more than a politically convenient attachment is something to develop at the table.

GM Note

This connection is a pressure point for Alaric — it ties his personal life to faction politics. If the crew's standing with the Hive becomes complicated, or if Alaric's Valchek problems escalate, Anya's position becomes relevant in multiple directions at once. She's also one degree from Lady Dalmer, which means she could be a route upward — or a liability.
