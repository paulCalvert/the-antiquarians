Brother Morrow

Former street preacher. He found something in the ghost field that answered back. The ritual always needs an officiant.

Who He Is

The Man
  • Former street preacher — charismatic, effective, the kind of speaker who fills a corner and then a room and then a square
  • Turned from faith healer to pact zealot as the bargains he brokered for others began to accumulate, as the ghost field answered his appeals in ways he could no longer pretend were metaphor
  • Calm now. The fire that made him a preacher didn’t go out; it got very quiet and very certain.
  • Speaks like an officiant at a legal funeral — everything is inevitable, recorded, and already settled. He is not convincing you. He is reading from the record.
  • One hand wrapped in old legal parchment, bound with black cord. Not a costume. It cannot be unwrapped. He does not explain it.
His Role in the Compact

He oversees major rites. He is the ceremonial voice at pact signings — he transforms what is legally binding into what is spiritually irreversible. Where Veleris Quill handles the documents, Morrow makes it final.

He interprets the Compact’s obligations when there is dispute. He prepares those who are about to commit fully. He is present at every significant ritual event — not to perform the magic, but to witness it with authority and lock it in place.

When He Appears

Not in Early Sessions

Morrow is an endgame figure. He surfaces when something is actually happening, not just being planned. His presence in a scene means the Compact is past preparation and into execution.

He does not fight. He performs. A crew that has been dealing with Veleris Quill’s paperwork and Nell Vask’s logistics for several sessions will feel the gear-shift when Morrow arrives. He is the ritual. He is what all of it was building toward.

GM Note

No personal agenda worth exploiting. No ambivalence, no doubt, no leverage point that comes from uncertainty. He is not deceived about what he serves — he understands it and chose it anyway. He is the specific kind of wrong that cannot be reached from outside. Some people are simply too far in. He is one of them.

That said: he is not cruel. He would be genuinely sorry if the crew suffered. He simply believes the obligation requires it. The sorrow and the certainty exist in him at the same time, without contradiction. That is what makes him effective and unreachable.

Connections

  • Veleris Quill — works in tandem; she prepares the legal ground, he performs the sealing rite
  • Setarra — what he serves; he understands this more clearly than most Compact members
  • The Compact — his home, his purpose; the Signatories defer to him on ritual matters
