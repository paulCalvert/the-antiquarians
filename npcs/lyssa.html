Roric

The Murder That Made Her Boss

Lyssa killed Roric Ś the previous Crows leader. This is known within the faction but the full circumstances are murky. Some old Crows are loyal to Roric's memory and don't accept her fully. The Hive wants to avenge him. Roric was also Djera Dalmer's former lover Ś which means Lyssa's rise is personal to the Hive's leader in a way that goes beyond factional politics.

Current Position

The Crows are in turmoil. Lyssa holds the top seat but not everyone accepts it. The Lampblacks and Red Sashes are jockeying for power in Crow's Foot amid the instability. There's also a question of possible Unseen influence in the faction's current direction.
