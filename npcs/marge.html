Marge

She runs the front shop. She is chronically underpaid. Everyone loves her anyway.

Role

  • Manager of the antique shop front business above the Sunken City
  • The crew's primary cover � any legitimate-facing interaction goes through Marge
  • Has almost certainly seen things she has carefully chosen not to ask about

Personality

  • Deadpan � her default expression is the same whether she's ringing up a sale or watching someone drag a body bag through the back room
  • Underpaid � she knows it, the crew knows it, nobody has fixed it
  • Beloved � the customers like her, the crew likes her, probably even the people who shouldn't like her like her. She is aware of this and does not consider it a substitute for a raise.

Notes

Marge is the kind of NPC who is most effective when used sparingly and consistently. She doesn't need a dramatic arc. She needs to be exactly herself, at exactly the wrong moments.
