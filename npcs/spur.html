The Wraiths

Spur

Not yet introduced. Manages the Wraiths' drop boxes. Very confident for someone who hasn't been tested yet.

Appearance

  • Young woman — clearly newer to this life than her attitude suggests
  • Shaved head
  • Piercing blue eyes — the kind that make people feel assessed
  • Wears a hood and cloth clothing — practical, anonymous, easy to move in
  • Carries herself like someone who expects to be taken seriously

Personality

The Cockiness

Spur is cocky — but it's the cockiness of inexperience, not competence. She's overcompensating. She knows how the world works in theory and she's proving it to herself. She hasn't been in a situation yet that pushed past what she could handle. That day is coming.

Role

  • Manages the Wraiths' drop boxes — the information and item exchange system
  • A logistics and coordination role, not front-line work
  • Knows the Wraiths' network in ways other members may not

Introduction Plan

GM Note

Not yet introduced to the crew. Planned introduction: Flint encounters her at a lock shop at some point. The lock shop setting is deliberate — it gives them common ground immediately. How that first meeting goes will shape the relationship.
