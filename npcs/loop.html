The Wraiths

Loop

The Wraiths' appraisal expert. He knows what things are worth. He is obsessive about it. His glasses keep sliding down his nose.

Appearance

  • Thin frame Ś not built for anything physical
  • Slicked back hair
  • Glasses that keep sliding down his nose Ś perpetually being pushed back up

Personality

  • Obsessive Ś once he starts evaluating something he can't stop until he knows its worth exactly
  • Moody Ś can flip from engaged to withdrawn; doesn't always explain why
  • The person you bring difficult items to; he will find the value or he'll explain precisely why it can't be determined

The Leviathan Maps

The Problem He Can't Solve

The Wraiths have leviathan maps Ś charts of the deep routes where leviathans travel, which is extremely valuable to hunters and traders. Loop knows exactly what they're worth. The problem isn't valuation; it's finding a buyer who has the coin and won't immediately turn around and sell the Wraiths out. The Hive is the most obvious buyer Ś and the most dangerous one.

Notes

Loop is the Wraiths member most likely to engage with the Antiquarians directly Ś they deal in artifacts and unusual items, which is his language. If the crew ever needs an appraisal on something genuinely strange, Loop is the person in Doskvol who might actually know what it is.
