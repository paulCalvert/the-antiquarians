Eisele

Leader of the Gondoliers. Serene, knowledgeable, and entirely unafraid. The crew hasn't met her yet.

Who She Is

  • Leader of the Gondoliers — the most influential figure in the faction
  • Short black hair, square jaw, broad frame — described as mannish
  • Serene in the way someone is when they've seen everything and aren't surprised anymore
  • Knowledgeable about the harbor's history, patterns, and what moves through it
  • Fearless — not reckless, but genuinely unintimidated

Status

The crew has not met Eisele yet. They know Lady Crane, who operates further down the Gondoliers hierarchy. If the harbor compact becomes a real issue — and it will — Eisele is the person who would know what to do about it. She may already know more than she's let on to anyone.

The Harbor

Gondoliers & The Compact

The Gondoliers perform the Tide Tithe and feel the harbor's rhythms more directly than any other faction. Eisele leads that relationship. Whether she knows the actual history behind the Tithesday superstition — or has pieces of it — is an open question for when the crew reaches her level.
