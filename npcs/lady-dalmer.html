Djera Dalmer

Dagger Isles urchin who clawed her way to the top of Doskvol's contraband trade. Elected leader of her guild. Her grief is personal, and it's driving faction policy.

Appearance

  • Somewhere in her 40s to 60s — hard to pin her age. Still striking.
  • Artificial golden nose — gleams in candlelight. The aesthetic leans toward her Dagger Isles roots.
  • Hair kept in a large, elaborate bun — architectural, deliberate. Hive-shaped, arguably.
  • Socialite presentation: dressed for influence, not practicality

Background

  • Grew up in the Dagger Isles as an urchin — no family wealth, no connections, nothing given
  • Worked her way to Doskvol and up through the contraband trade
  • Became the elected leader of the Hive — not by inheritance or takeover, but by the guild's choice
  • Was lovers with Roric, the previous Crows leader, before his death

Roric & the Crows

The Personal Thread

Roric was killed by Lyssa, who took the Crows from him. For Djera, this isn't just a factional concern — it's a loss. The Hive's goal to avenge Roric is her goal. Karth Orris is the instrument of that plan, and he has his own reasons for wanting Lyssa gone. This is a volatile combination.

Possible Conflict

Djera's personal grief and the Hive's factional goals aren't perfectly aligned. What's good revenge isn't always what's good politics. If the crew gets tangled in the Hive-Crows conflict, they may find themselves mediating between Djera's emotions and her better judgment.

Power & Position

  • Big money and deep investments across Doskvol's commercial interests
  • Possibly on the city council — her influence is at that level
  • Avoids anything supernatural or superstitious — this is a firm Hive policy and a personal trait
  • Has not been introduced to the crew directly; Anya is the working contact
