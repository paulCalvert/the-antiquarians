Gondoliers — Second

Griggs

Chief Whisper of the Gondoliers. Blind eyes, broken nose, messy hair. Haunted and ruthless. The crew hasn't met him yet.

Who He Is

  • Second in command of the Gondoliers, directly under Eisele
  • The faction's chief Whisper — their occult specialist and intelligence operative
  • Blind eyes — clouded over, doesn't see the way other people see
  • Broken nose, messy hair — not someone who worries about appearances
  • Haunted: the weight of what a Whisper sees shows on him
  • Ruthless: the Gondoliers have survived this long partly because of him

Status

The crew has not met Griggs yet. Lady Crane is their point of contact within the Gondoliers — Griggs operates at a different level. He's the person the faction sends when something needs to be known or dealt with quietly.

The Whisper Angle

What He Might Know

A Whisper embedded in the faction most attuned to the harbor's rhythms is not someone who lacks context. Griggs has been reading the ghost field around Doskvol's waterways for years. If the compact is degrading, he would feel it — and he may already have suspicions about why.
