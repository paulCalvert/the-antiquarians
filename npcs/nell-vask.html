Ash-Hand — Senior Notary

Nell Vask

Field coordinator. Gets things done. Keeps all her contract cuts on the same palm.

Who She Is

The Operative
  • Senior Notary — the Compact’s operational backbone in the field
  • Practical, capable, dry humor that never quite reaches warmth
  • Looks like a mid-level clerk in a legal firm. The danger doesn’t show until it’s relevant.
  • Keeps all her contract cuts on the same palm — each layered over the last, a record written in scar tissue. She doesn’t hide them.
  • Does not ask about the bigger picture. Does not need to know. Her job is logistics, not philosophy.
How She Operates

She is the person cells call when something is going wrong and they need someone who actually knows what they’re doing. She has seen enough to have contingencies for most problems and patience for the rest.

She will negotiate. She will trade information. She will make a deal if one is genuinely on the table. She will not be threatened — she has been in this network long enough that being threatened only tells her how much pressure you’re under.

Connection to Harrow

She Would Recognize Him

Harrow worked under or alongside Nell Vask during his time in the Compact. He knows her by face and name. She knows him by the same.

What she does not know: whether he is active, dormant, or defected. That question, if it comes up between them, is the entire scene. She would not panic. She would assess. And then she would decide what his status means for her current operation.

GM Note

Recurring rival. The most likely Compact operative to appear on scores as the crew’s specific obstacle — not a boss-level encounter, but a competent professional doing her job who happens to be in the way. She can be dealt with, negotiated with, or turned against another cell if the crew is clever about it. Her loyalty is professional, not ideological — the Compact pays well and has useful resources. That is a leverage point.

Connections

  • The Ash-Hand Compact — Senior Notary; field coordinator; the operational layer between cells and Inner Ring direction
  • Harrow — former colleague; knows his face; does not know his current status
  • Veleris Quill — works above her; Nell handles field logistics while Quill handles the long game
